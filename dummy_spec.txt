SECTION 05120 - STRUCTURAL STEEL

PART 1 - GENERAL

1.1 SUMMARY
A. Section Includes:
   1. Structural steel framing.
   2. Grout.

1.5 QUALITY ASSURANCE
A. Fire Rating: All structural steel columns must have a fire rating of at least 120 minutes.
B. Connections: All bolted connections shall use ASTM A325 high-strength bolts.
C. Fabrication: Fabricator must be AISC Certified.
